# PROCUREMENT INTEGRITY ACT (41 U.S.C. § 2105)

## What It Prohibits

**Violations of fair competition and conflicts of interest in federal procurement**

### Key Violations:
1. **Bid Rigging**: Collusion between vendors or with officials
2. **Insider Information**: Using non-public bid data
3. **Conflicts of Interest**: Officials benefiting from contracts
4. **Revolving Door**: Officials joining vendors they awarded to

## Fraud Indicators

**Bid Rigging Red Flags:**
- Same vendor winning >30% of contracts in jurisdiction
- Bid prices suspiciously similar (within 1-2%)
- Vendors taking turns winning (rotation pattern)
- Few bidders despite competitive market

**Sole-Source Abuse:**
- Sole-source for readily available items
- Emergency justification for non-urgent needs
- Splitting contracts to avoid competitive thresholds

**Conflict of Interest:**
- Official awards contract, then joins vendor
- Vendor employs official's family members
- Vendor located in official's district

## Legal Thresholds

**Prosecution requires:**
- Evidence of coordination or inside information
- Benefit to official or immediate family
- Harm to competitive process

**Penalties:**
- Contract cancellation
- Debarment from future contracts
- Criminal prosecution (up to 5 years)
- Civil penalties

## Prosecuted Examples

**US v. United Technologies (2015)**
- Paid consulting fees to official's consulting firm
- Official influenced contract decisions
- Settlement: $75M + debarment

**US v. Northrop Grumman (2009)**
- Hired official immediately after contract award
- Official had influenced procurement
- Settlement: $12.5M

## Application to Our Analysis

**When to flag for Procurement Integrity Act:**
- Vendor wins >30% of contracts in sample
- Sole-source contract for standard items
- Political donation → contract award <90 days
- Vendor concentration + price inflation combo

**Confidence Levels:**
- High (90%+): Multiple indicators + donation timeline
- Medium (70-90%): Concentration pattern only
- Low (<70%): Need employment/family connection data
